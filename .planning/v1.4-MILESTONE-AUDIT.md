### Integration Issues

**QUAL-01 PARTIAL** — `DeviceAction.device_type` in `packages/rig/src/rig/engine/plan/models.py:10` is typed `str`, not `DeviceType`. Two production call sites assign raw string literals:
- `compute.py:93`: `device_type="analog"`
- `apply.py:165`: `device_type="controller"`

The Phase 20 VERIFICATION grep checked `== "analog"` comparisons (none found), but these are *assignment* sites — they escape the grep pattern. The field is `str` so Pydantic accepts them silently.

**TYPE-03 PARTIAL** — `packages/rig-morningstar/src/rig_morningstar/device.py:33`: `presets: list[Any]` not typed against `Preset` Protocol. Three of four plugin devices are correct.

### Warning (non-blocking)
